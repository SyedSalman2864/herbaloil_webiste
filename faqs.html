What is Navuedha Ayurvedic Hair Oil?

Navuedha Ayurvedic Hair Oil is a 100% natural hair oil designed to tackle common hair problems such as hair fall, dandruff, premature greying, and baldness.

How do I use this hair oil?

Massage the oil gently into your scalp and hair roots. Leave it on for at least an hour or overnight for the best results. Wash it off with a mild shampoo.

How often should I use it?

For optimal results, use the oil 2-3 times a week as part of your regular hair care routine.

Are there any side effects?

Navuedha Ayurvedic Hair Oil is made from natural ingredients and is safe for all hair types. However, we recommend conducting a patch test before the first use.

When can I expect to see results?

Visible improvements such as reduced hair fall and better scalp health can typically be seen within 15 days of regular use.

Is it suitable for all hair types?

Yes, our hair oil is formulated to work effectively on all hair types, including dry, oily, and combination hair.
